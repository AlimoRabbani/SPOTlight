Device Training

Your device is controlled based on the following equation: PPV = a*PMV + b. In this equation, PMV is Predicted Mean Vote and is calculated based on temperature, humidity, air velocity, metabolic rate, and attire. Since we are not measuring some of these parameters, we assume fixed values for them and adjust the function using "a" and "b" coefficients and calculate PPV. Currently, a = {{ device.device_parameter_a|round(2, 'floor') }} and b = {{ device.device_parameter_b|round(2, 'floor') }} for your device. If you think that your device is not calibrated, hit the train button below and you will have 24 hours to vote and train it.
