Who is doing this?

A group of researchers at University of Waterloo started this project. The project belongs to ISS4E Lab and is supervised by Prof. Keshav. Currently, Alimohammad Rabbani, Milad Khaki, and Costin Ograda-Bratu are involved in the project. We are open to collaborate with other universities and groups on this project too.

What is this website?

This website is created primarily for users to control and monitor their SPOTlight instances. It also provides admin tools to monitor the deployment and start troubleshooting if something goes wrong. The website also provides a brief description of SPOTlight for people who are interested in this kind of research.

How can I help?

If you find yourself interested in this project, you can always contact us at amrabban@uwaterloo.ca. We are looking forward to your comments and suggestions and we will be more than happy to have you on board if you are interested.
